Our Books

Ryde Persian School uses the comprehensive, up-to-date, and engaging ‘Aasaan Aamooz’ educational books, designed to suit students’ ages and learning levels, from preschool through to the early primary years.

These books focus on both language and cultural connections, helping students learn Persian while strengthening their ties to their cultural identity.

The series is the result of years of experience, research, and commitment to preserving the mother language in multilingual environments, and serves as a valuable resource for families, teachers, and anyone interested in Persian language education.
