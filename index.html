Welcome to Ryde Persian School

We are delighted to welcome you and your children to Ryde Persian School and to our Open Day. Ryde Persian School was established with the aim of teaching the Persian language while nurturing children’s cultural identity. We believe that a mother tongue is a vital bridge between generations and plays an essential role in building confidence, identity, and a strong sense of belonging.

At Ryde Persian School, we are committed to creating a safe, supportive, and joyful learning environment where students not only develop their Persian language skills but also form meaningful connections with Persian culture and traditions.

We sincerely thank parents for their continued support and partnership, and we look forward to sharing this educational journey together. On behalf of our teachers, management committee, and most importantly our students, we warmly welcome you to Ryde Persian School. Kind regards, Forouza Soltani (Principal Ryde Persian School)
