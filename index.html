Welcome to Ryde Persian School

A nonprofit school teaching Persian to children and adults under the guidance of the Iranian Educational & Cultural Center.

At the Persian school, we nurture confident, curious learners, while celebrating Persian language and culture. For this reason, families, children, and adults eager to learn Persian are always welcome.

Meet our passionate teachers on Saturday mornings during the school term!

Principal’s Message

We are delighted to welcome you and your children to Ryde Persian School and to our Open Day. Ryde Persian School was established with the aim of teaching the Persian language while nurturing children’s cultural identity.

We believe that a mother language is a vital bridge between generations and plays an essential role in building confidence, identity, and a strong sense of belonging.

At Ryde Persian School, we are committed to creating a safe, supportive, and joyful learning environment where students not only develop their language skills but also form meaningful connections with Persian culture and traditions.

We sincerely thank parents for their continued support and partnership, and we look forward to sharing this educational journey together.

Frouza Soltani
Principal, Ryde Persian School

Ryde Persian School is a non-for-profit organisation aiming to teach the Persian language to all ages. It operates under the umbrella of “Iranian Educational and Cultural Centre Inc.”
